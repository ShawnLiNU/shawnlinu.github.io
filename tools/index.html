Apple Activity & Health Data Viewer
Visualize Apple Health exports directly in the browser. Load .gpx workout files to see interactive maps, elevation profiles, and speed charts. Load ECG .csv files for clinical-style waveform display, R-peak detection, HRV analysis, and rhythm classification.

EECE 5554 1D & 2D Interactive
Kalman Filter Visualizer
Interactive 1D and 2D Kalman filter demo for Robot Sensing & Navigation. Tune process noise Q, measurement noise R, and initial uncertainty P₀ with sliders or direct keyboard input — watch the filter estimate converge against noisy measurements in real time. Includes step-by-step playback, live gain and RMSE readouts, and an equation reference panel.
